```If people do not believe that mathematics is simple, it is only because they do not realize how complicated life is.```


Explanation:
-->The statement suggests that people may perceive mathematics as complex because they fail to recognize the complexities inherent in everyday life. It implies that mathematics, in comparison, is actually a simpler and more structured domain. The statement highlights the notion that mathematics provides a logical framework for understanding and solving problems, while life itself presents numerous intricacies and uncertainties. Ultimately, it underscores the idea that mathematics offers a sense of clarity and order in the face of life's complexities.
